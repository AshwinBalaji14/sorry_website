A VERSE FROM THE STARS

Ode to the Girl Who Is Magic

In a world of ordinary things,
she arrived like a spell from ancient pages —
eyes that hold the depth of oceans,
a heart that crafts its own galaxies.

She weaves wonder with her hands —
bracelets born of light and intention,
each bead a whispered incantation,
each knot a binding of beautiful dreams.

She rides through life with fearless grace,
and soon — oh soon — the wind shall be hers,
the open road shall know her name,
the bike beneath her, freedom pure.

She reads the worlds within the worlds,
she plants her bouquets like prayers,
she is princess and witch and wanderer —
and I am blessed beyond all measure
simply to know that she exists.

May every breath come easy now,
may every year bring strength untold —
you are not just a girl who is lovely,
you are a universe, barely begun to unfold.

— written in starlight, signed in purple 💜
